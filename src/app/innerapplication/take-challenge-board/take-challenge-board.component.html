CodeAma challenges guidelines
To start the challenge you must have the following:
  1. You must have a working account in codesandbox
The guidelines are here:
  1. Click on Edit sandbox button in the sandbox ui provided
  2. The link will direct you into codesandbox
  3. After that, in codesandbox at the upper right hand side click Fork to make this project yours
  4. After forking the project click share button to share it to us
  5. Choose embed
  6. Copy the embed code provided in the appearing modal at the left hand side
  7. Paste that embed codes in the input box provided to your on this page
  8. Click on submit button to submit your project embed to us
Where to code from
  1. You must write all your codes in the sandbox you've forked at codesandbox site
  2. To see your saved changes on our site, refresh this page
  3. Keep in mind that the changes you will make in your codes at our page will not be saved (everyting should be done in codesandbox)

We wish you good luck while you develop your programming skills through our coding challenges
